At Houston Chess School, we're more than just a place to play chess — we're a community built on growth, curiosity, and excellence. Based in Houston, we specialize in teaching students of all ages and skill levels, from absolute beginners to tournament-ready competitors. Our certified coaches, including those who've trained with world-class players, bring passion and expertise to every lesson. Whether in group classes, private sessions, or school programs, our goal is simple: to unlock each student's potential through the power of chess.


Our teaching philosophy blends structured learning with creativity and critical thinking. We believe that chess isn't just a game — it's a tool for building life skills like focus, patience, and problem-solving. That's why we create a supportive, challenging environment where every student feels encouraged to grow at their own pace. Whether your goal is to compete, improve, or just have fun, we're here to guide you every move of the way.

Services

1-on-1 Coaching

Get personalized, one-on-one training with a certified coach. Perfect for players looking to accelerate their growth, sharpen their tactics, or prepare for upcoming tournaments.

Group Lessons

Our group classes offer an energetic, collaborative environment where students learn together and grow together. Led by certified coaches, these sessions focus on building a strong foundation in chess — from basic rules and piece movement to opening principles and tactical patterns.

Advanced Sessions

For serious players ready to take their skills to the next level, our advanced sessions dive deep into high-level strategy, endgame mastery, tournament preparation, and classic game analysis.
